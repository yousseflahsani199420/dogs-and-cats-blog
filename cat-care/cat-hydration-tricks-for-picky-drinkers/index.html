Advanced Routine Optimization#

Once your baseline routine works, optimize in small layers. Keep session timing predictable, use environment cues, and rotate rewards to maintain motivation without overstimulation.

Document one behavior goal and one health goal each week. This dual focus keeps progress balanced and prevents tunnel vision on a single symptom.

Troubleshooting Scenarios#

If progress stops, review sleep quality, meal timing, hydration, and daily stimulation levels. Basic care variables often explain behavior setbacks.

If your pet regresses after improvement, return to the last stable routine for 3 to 5 days before introducing any new adjustment.

If the issue appears only in one room or one time block, treat it as an environmental trigger and redesign that specific context first.
