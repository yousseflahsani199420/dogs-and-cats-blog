## Our Mission

At **PetZone**, we publish practical and trustworthy guidance for dog and cat owners. Our mission is to make pet care easier through clear steps, useful checklists, and real-world routines you can apply immediately.

## What We Cover

- **Dog Care** - Grooming, hygiene, seasonal routines, and daily comfort.
- **Cat Care** - Indoor setup, litter habits, enrichment, and stress reduction.
- **Pet Health** - Symptom awareness, first aid basics, and vet-readiness.
- **Pet Food** - Portioning, ingredient quality, and diet transitions.
- **Pet Training** - Positive reinforcement plans for behavior and obedience.

## Editorial Approach

We focus on practical, owner-friendly content:

- Short steps that are easy to follow at home
- Safety-first recommendations with clear warnings
- Guides organized by common problems and life stage
- Internal links that help you continue learning quickly

## Disclaimer

All content is for informational purposes and does not replace veterinary diagnosis or treatment. Always consult your veterinarian for medical concerns specific to your pet.
