Our **Pet Health** section helps you spot early warning signs and take smart first steps at home before your veterinary visit.

## What You Will Find Here

- Symptom checklists for common dog and cat issues
- First-aid basics for non-emergency situations
- Recovery routines after procedures and illness
- Hydration, mobility, and chronic tracking tips
- Emergency-prep guides for faster decisions

## Important Medical Reminder

Use these articles for education and preparation only. For severe symptoms, breathing problems, collapse, persistent vomiting, blood in stool, seizures, or sudden pain, contact a veterinarian immediately.

For nutrition-related prevention, visit [Pet Food](/pet-food/). For behavior-related stress support, visit [Pet Training](/pet-training/).
